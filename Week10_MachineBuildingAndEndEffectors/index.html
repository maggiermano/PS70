Week 10-12: Machine Building and End Effectors

This week the assignment was to work with a team to build a 2+ DoF drawing machine. The team was encouraged to heavily modify the example hardware and software. At a minimum, it should implement a new end-effector. Drawing machines would need to demonstrate: - Ability to calibrate motor position - Ability to draw a perfect circle 6" and mark its center point
